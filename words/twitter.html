General Twitter Slang

acc - account alt - alternate account avi - avatar, profile picture bang, blow up, pop off - (of a tweet) to get a lot of interactions banger - a tweet that gets a lot of interactions byf - before you follow cbyf - read caard before you follow cc - a CuriousCat or message on it, OR content creator, OR comfort character, OR carbon copy: mentioning someone who you want to see your tweet discourse twitter - a Twitter environment full of debate and arguments dfi - don't follow if dm - direct message, a way of privately messaging dms open, open dms - user has opted into allowing anyone to direct message them dms closed, closed dms - user not allowed everyone to direct message them dn - display name dnf - do not follow dnfi - do not follow if dni - do not interact, do not follow/comment/message etc. if x drafts - tweets that weren't sent but saved by the user for later f4f - follow for follow, user will follow back if you follow fan acc - an account used to be a fan of someone or something fav, fave - favorite, now likes fb - follow back fleet - a disappearing story on Twitter, similar to Instagram stories gc - group chat __gc - an acronym for the name of a group chat, with other letters in place of the underscores gdm - group direct message, a group chat gimmick account - an account with a theme, or gimmick ia - inactive, or instant accept ib, icb - profile icon by ic - profile icon ifb - I follow back, user will follow back if you follow layout - the theme and name of an account lrt - last retweet, used to tell you to refer to the last thing someone retweeted matching - having the same or similar profile pictures or layouts mbf - must be following notifs, notis - opted-in notifications that are sent when an account posts notification squad, noti gang - a group of users that opted in to receiving notifications for when an account posts oc - original content ooc - out of character, or out of context op - original poster, original post parody - an account pretending to be someone or something that it is not as a joke or character pfp - profile picture priv - private account usually used for more personal matters to private, privated - to set your account to private, so that only followers can see your tweets qt, qrt - quote tweet, or retweet with comment ratio'd, ratioed - to have a reply to your tweet get more likes ratio - (positive) to get more likes on a tweet than another, or (negative) to get more replies or quote tweets than likes reply guy - a person that replies to many posts, especially women's reply guying - to reply to many posts rl - real life rl acc - an account for your real life personality and real life things rt - retweet rted, rt'd, rtd - retweeted sb - soft block, blocking and unblocking to have someone unfollow you and to prevent direct messages ss - screenshot subtweeting - tweeting about somebody without mentioning their name thread - a chain of tweet replies tl - timeline trends - hashtags or phrases that are being talked about, show up on trending tab Twitter guy - the unknown person that writes the descriptions for trends unf - unfollow untag - to remove from the list of people mentioned when replying vent, vent acc - an account used for complaining and personal issues @ - Twitter username, or handle @ing, @'d - to mention a Twitter username (@example) @ me next time - said when it is obvious someone is talking about them, usually sarcastic
