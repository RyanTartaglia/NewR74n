General Twitter Slang

acc - account alt - alternate account avi - avatar bang, blow up, pop off - (of a tweet) to get a lot of interactions banger - a tweet that gets a lot of interactions cc - a CuriousCat or message on it, OR carbon copy: mentioning someone who you want to see your tweet dm - direct message dni - do not interact f4f - follow for follow fav, fave - favorite, now likes fb - follow back fleet - a disappearing story on Twitter, similar to Instagram stories gc - group chat gimmick account - an account with a theme, or gimmick ifb - I follow back layout - the theme and name of an account lrt - last retweet notifs, notis - opted-in notifications that are sent when an account posts notification squad, noti gang - a group of users that opted in to receiving notifications for when an account posts op - original poster, original post pfp - profile picture priv - private account usually used for more personal matters qt, qrt - quote tweet, or retweet with comment ratio'd, ratioed - to have a reply to your tweet get more likes ratio - (positive) to get more likes on a tweet than another, or (negative) to get more replies or quote tweets than likes reply guy - a person that replies to many posts, especially women's reply guying - to reply to many posts rt - retweet sb - soft block, blocking and unblocking to prevent direct messages subtweeting - tweeting about somebody without mentioning their name thread - a chain of tweet replies tl - timeline trends - hashtags or phrases that are being talked about, show up on trending tab Twitter guy - the unknown person that writes the descriptions for trends untag - to remove from the list of people mentioned when replying
