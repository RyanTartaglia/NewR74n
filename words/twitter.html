General Twitter Slang

acc - account alt - alternate account avi - avatar, profile picture bang, blow up, pop off - (of a tweet) to get a lot of interactions banger - a tweet that gets a lot of interactions byf - before you follow cc - a CuriousCat or message on it, OR content creator, OR carbon copy: mentioning someone who you want to see your tweet dm - direct message, a way of privately messaging dms open, open dms - user has opted into allowing anyone to direct message them dms closed, closed dms - user not allowed everyone to direct message them dn - display name dni - do not interact, do not follow/comment/message etc. if x drafts - tweets that weren't sent but saved by the user for later f4f - follow for follow, user will follow back if you follow fan acc - an account used to be a fan of someone or something fav, fave - favorite, now likes fb - follow back fleet - a disappearing story on Twitter, similar to Instagram stories gc - group chat __gc - an acronym for the name of a group chat, with other letters in place of the underscores gdm - group direct message, a group chat gimmick account - an account with a theme, or gimmick ic - profile icon ia - inactive, or instant accept ifb - I follow back, user will follow back if you follow layout - the theme and name of an account lrt - last retweet, used to tell you to refer to the last thing someone retweeted mbf - must be following notifs, notis - opted-in notifications that are sent when an account posts notification squad, noti gang - a group of users that opted in to receiving notifications for when an account posts oc - original content op - original poster, original post parody - an account pretending to be someone or something that it is not as a joke or character pfp - profile picture priv - private account usually used for more personal matters qt, qrt - quote tweet, or retweet with comment ratio'd, ratioed - to have a reply to your tweet get more likes ratio - (positive) to get more likes on a tweet than another, or (negative) to get more replies or quote tweets than likes reply guy - a person that replies to many posts, especially women's reply guying - to reply to many posts rl - real life rl acc - an account for your real life personality and real life things rt - retweet sb - soft block, blocking and unblocking to have someone unfollow you and to prevent direct messages subtweeting - tweeting about somebody without mentioning their name thread - a chain of tweet replies tl - timeline trends - hashtags or phrases that are being talked about, show up on trending tab Twitter guy - the unknown person that writes the descriptions for trends untag - to remove from the list of people mentioned when replying @ - Twitter username, or handle @ing - to mention a Twitter username (@example) @ me next time - said when it is obvious someone is talking about them, usually sarcastic

General Stan Twitter Slang

anisd - anitwitter selfie day asf, af - as fuck bio chain - multiple accounts with consecutive lyrics in their bio bop - good song cancel culture - trend of boycotting people or things cancel - to boycott cancelled, canceled, over - boycotted carrd - a page on carrd.co chile - child comfort character - a character that someone likes and feels comforted by d word, d*e - die d worded - deactivated eye-, I- - speechless fancam - recording of a person performing flop - to get less interactions on a tweet than suspected going dark - turning your profile picture and header black in shame hc - headcanon, someone's personal interpretation of canon hunny - honey irl, irls - people in real life keysmash - string of random letters keysmashing - typing keys at random kin - a character which one kins king - someone highly respected kinlist, kin list - a list of characters someone kins kinnie - someone that kins a character kinning - to believe you're similar to a character locals - people outside of the community moots, mutuals - users that you follow that follow you back noomf - not one of my followers oomf - one of my followers, one of my friends, sometimes just "followers" or a sound similar to oof oomirls - one of my in real life friends oop-, and I oop - caught off guard periodt - period, strong emphasis queen - an idol, someone looked up to s worded, sus'd, sussed - suspended selca - a selfie selfie day, selca day - a hashtag for a certain community encouraging members to post selfies. (e.g., #dttwtselfieday, #anitwtselfieday) shade - trash talking ship - a real or fictional relationship between two people sis - sister skinny, skinny legend - glamorous and talented regardless of weight sksksk - a common laughing keysmash slay, go off, pop off - to do very well stan list - a list of people or groups someone stans stan - fan, originally stalker fan, obsessive fan stanning - to be a fan of a person or group stew - opposite of tea stewen - old gossip or drama stream - listen to, watch tanking - underperforming tea - gossip or drama tsd - Thai selca day ub, ult - ultimate bias, favorite person or group wbk - we been knew wig snatched, wig gone, wig flew - shocked yas - yes
