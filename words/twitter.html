General Twitter Slang

alt - alternate account avi - avatar bang, blow up, pop off - (of a tweet) to get a lot of interactions banger - a tweet that gets a lot of interactions cc - carbon copy, mentioning someone who you want to see your tweet dm - direct message dni - do not interact f4f - follow for follow fav - favorite, now likes fb - follow back fleet - a disappearing story on Twitter, similar to Instagram stories gc - group chat ifb - I follow back layout - the theme and name of an account lrt - last retweet op - original poster, original post pfp - profile picture priv - private account usually used for more personal matters qt, qrt - quote tweet, or retweet with comment ratio'd, ratioed - to have a reply to your tweet get more likes ratio - (positive) to get more likes on a tweet than another, or (negative) to get more replies or quote tweets than likes rt - retweet sb - soft block, blocking and unblocking to prevent direct messages subtweeting - tweeting about somebody without mentioning their name tl - timeline untag - to remove from the list of people mentioned when replying

General Stan Twitter Slang

anisd - anitwitter selfie day asf, af - as fuck bio chain - multiple accounts with consecutive lyrics in their bio bop - good song cancel culture - trend of boycotting people or things cancel - to boycott cancelled, canceled, over - boycotted carrd - a page on carrd.co chile - child d word, d*e - die d worded - deactivated eye- - I-, speechless fancam - recording of a person performing flop - to get less interactions on a tweet than suspected going dark - turning your profile picture and header black in shame hunny - honey irl, irls - users that know you in real life irls - people in real life keysmash - string of random letters keysmashing - typing keys at random kin - a character which one kins king - someone highly respected kinlist, kin list - a list of characters someone kins kinnie - someone that kins a character kinning - to believe you're similar to a character locals - people outside the community moots, mutuals - users that you follow that follow you back noomf - not one of my followers oomf - one of my followers, one of my friends oomirls - one of my in real life friends oop-, and I oop - caught off guard periodt - period, strong emphasis queen - an idol, someone looked up to s worded, sus'd, sussed - suspended selca - a selfie selfie day, selca day - a hashtag for a certain community encouraging members to post selfies. i.e. #dttwtselfieday, #anitwtselfieday shade - trash talking ship - a real or fictional relationship between two people sis - sister skinny, skinny legend - glamorous and talented regardless of weight sksksk - a common laughing keysmash slay, go off, pop off - to do very well stan list - a list of people or groups someone stans stan - fan, originally stalker fan, obsessive fan stanning - to be a fan of a person or group stew - opposite of tea stewen - old gossip or drama stream - listen to, watch tanking - underperforming tea - gossip or drama tsd - Thai selca day ub - ultimate bias wig snatched, wig gone, wig flew - shocked yas - yes
